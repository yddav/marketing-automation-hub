Frequently Asked Questions

What is FINDERR?

FINDERR is Android's first emergency wallpaper system. When your phone is lost, activate emergency mode remotely via SMS or web dashboard - your lockscreen transforms into a contact billboard displaying your emergency contact info.

Why do you need beta testers?

Google Play requires at least 12 testers using the app for 14 days before we can publish to production. This validates app quality and helps us find bugs before public launch.

What's the commitment?

14 days of testing. Install FINDERR from Google Play Testing track, use the features, test emergency scenarios, and provide feedback via email. Takes ~15 minutes total over 14 days.

Is it really free?

Yes! Beta testing is completely free. After launch, you'll get 50% off forever ($3.50/month instead of $6.99) as a thank you for helping us.

What if I find bugs?

Perfect! That's what beta testing is for. Email us at beta@untrapd.com with any bugs, issues, or suggestions. Your feedback makes FINDERR better for everyone.

When does beta testing start?

Immediately after you sign up! We'll send your Google Play Closed Testing invite within 24 hours. Download the app and start testing right away.

What happens after 14 days?

We apply for production access. Once approved by Google (1-7 days), FINDERR launches publicly. Your beta access continues with your 50% lifetime discount locked in.
